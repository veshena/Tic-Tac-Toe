Welcome to Veshena's Tic-Tac-Toe Game

Game Rules

This game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares. You are V, and your friend (or the computer in this case) is G. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares. The first player to get 3 marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) is the winner. When all 9 squares are full, the game is over.
